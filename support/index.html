Frequently Asked

How do I open a B2B wholesale account? expand_more

Email Sales@nexautogear.com with your company name, country, business type (distributor, shop, fleet), and estimated monthly volume. Our team approves most accounts within one business day.

What are the minimum order quantities (MOQ)? expand_more

Wheel MOQ is typically 50 units per SKU for standard catalog items. Custom finishes or private label runs require 200+ units. TPMS sensors start at 100 units per model. Contact Sales for mixed-SKU orders.

What is the production lead time for wheels? expand_more

In-stock catalog wheels ship within 3–5 business days. Production orders for custom finishes or sizes are 4–6 weeks. Private label orders with custom branding require 6–8 weeks. All timelines exclude shipping transit.

Do you offer Net-30 or credit terms? expand_more

Net-30 terms are available for established accounts with 3+ completed orders. New accounts typically start on 30% deposit / 70% before shipment. Letter of Credit (LC) is accepted for orders above USD 50,000.

How do I get TPMS sensor tech support? expand_more

Use the live chat widget (bottom right) for immediate TPMS programming guidance, or email Support@nexautogear.com with your vehicle make/model/year and sensor model. We respond within 2 hours during business hours.

Is there a warranty on AegisRim wheels? expand_more

Yes. All AegisRim forged wheels carry a 2-year structural warranty against manufacturing defects. Warranty claims are handled by our support team. Evidence of damage by road hazard, improper installation, or overload is not covered.
