How We Started

Built From
Real Industry Experience

NEXAutogear was built by people who spent nearly two decades inside the TPMS industry — not selling it from the outside. Starting in firmware engineering and moving into hands-on sensor validation, our team logged thousands of field test hours across vehicle platforms before ever launching a product of our own.

Through years working with Max TPMS tools and supporting distributor networks globally, we saw the same problem repeatedly: reliable, factory-direct TPMS and wheel supply was inaccessible to quality-focused distributors outside Asia without navigating opaque channels and OEM-scale minimums.

In 2025 we launched our own product line and partnered with AegisRim — a certified precision forging manufacturer in Taiwan — to bring that access to B2B partners worldwide with flexible MOQs and multilingual support.

Today, NEXAutogear serves active B2B accounts across North America, Europe, Australia, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East.

Quality Standards

Certifications & Standards

JWL

Japan Light Alloy Wheel

Mandatory for Japanese market. Tests rotary bending, radial fatigue, and 13° impact. All AegisRim catalog wheels compliant.

VIA

Vehicle Inspection Assoc.

Third-party JWL verification body. VIA mark confirms independent testing compliance beyond manufacturer self-declaration.

TÜV

TÜV Rheinland

German technical inspection authority. Required for European distribution. Covers dimensional accuracy, material grade, and load ratings.

IATF

IATF 16949 QMS

Automotive quality management system standard. Our manufacturing facility is IATF 16949 certified — the highest QMS benchmark in the auto industry.
